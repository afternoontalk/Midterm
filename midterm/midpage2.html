History of Modern Central Wuhan

In 1950, one year after the establishment of People's Republic of China, Communist Party School of Hubei province was established besides the central Park of Wuhan.
In 1964, Wuhan Foreign Languages School was established on the same street as the Party School as one of the first public schools in China to teach foreign languages.

This heighborhood had gradually became one of the most promising neighborhood in Wuhan.

As a respond to city governments proposal in developing this neighborhood to a pedestrain street around 1990s,

numerous merchandise flood in and light up this one-mile long street with international brands, restaurants, and hotels, all the way from north to south.

WanSongYuan


is the name of this street, it means "the garden for ten thousands pine tree". It was named after a glorious garden built by two wealthy merchants back in early 1900s. Now, over nearly a century, it becomes memories of youth of a new generation.

The early decade of the 21th century was the golden age of the Wan street. The energy brought by students burrowed into every nook and cranny and numerous owners have struck their first gold and built their families around the neighborhood.
On August 7, 2010, Cai's Beef noodle shop across from the Party School caught on a fire. The chef's emergency treatment averted a much larger explosion.
In 2014, city government includes the Wan street in the renovation agenda.
In 2016, the Hubei film studio was included in demolition agenda as well.
In 2018, regulations prohibited the rental of military properties for commercial use.
From Januray 23rd, 2020, to Apirl 8th, Wuhan lockdown for 76 days to prevent the spread of COVID-19. The renovation and demolition agenda has been delayed.
In September 2021, The Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council issued the Double Reduction Policy, prohibits any form of out-of-school classes, including subject and interest classes, to reduce students burden. Two-third of class and studio on the street was closed.
In 2023, denovation and construction is still going on in the Wan street. The Wuhan Foreign Languages School held its 25th Fall Sports Competition from 3 years resume.

The owner for bookstore and coffee house from early century is still here while others has shutdown. As long as the students are there, they will prove then a cheap and tasty option.
